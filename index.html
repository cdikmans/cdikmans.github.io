biography

my practice aims to investigate transformation processes and facilitate spaces for collaborative learning and designing pasts, presents and futures. With a academic background in sociology, architectural- and speculative-design, I am passionate about understanding how (social) structures are embedded in ever-changing global environments and how we can collectively react to ongoing (climate) emergencies.
I believe that exchange and discussion are key to opening up new possibilities of seeing and thinking and promoting heterogeneous coexistences.

please reach out to me via e-mail, find me on instagram or connect with me on linkedin.
